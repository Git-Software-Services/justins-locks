Ignition Switch Not working?

Is your vehicle dashboard reporting a "bad key" error message? We specialize in repairing car ingnitions - something most locksmiths won't touch. Taking your vehicle to a mechanic will usually result in the expensive replacement of your ignition switch. The most common vehicles prone to ignition or key issues are Honda's 2003 and up and Dodge 2000 and up. CHevrolet keys have had issues from 97 and up. All these vehicles would have problems when trying to turn the key to start the car.

Dodge and Chrysler 2008 and up don't neccessarily have an issue turning but the keys can all of a sudden stop working in you not being able to start the car. In this case a message will come on the dash screen saying "bad key".
