    Bump Proof Locks

    Lock bumping, also referred to as key bumping is an attack technique using specially cut keys, known as bump keys, that can defeat conventional pin and tumbler door locks.

    In order for a lock to be truly bump-proof, or pick resistant, it must have one of two things:
  • No keyhole
  • A locking mechanism that is designed to be specifically bump proof
